What We Treat

Comprehensive Outdoor Pest Solutions

From surface insects to underground pests, our targeted programs defend every layer of your property — so you can enjoy your yard without worry.

Flea & Tick Control

Protect your family and pets with targeted flea and tick treatments applied to your lawn and yard. West Tennessee's warm, humid summers create ideal conditions for these pests to thrive.

  • Targeted spray treatments to lawn and shrub areas
  • Rapid knockdown of active flea and tick populations
  • Preventive barrier applications
  • Safe for pets and family after drying (typically 1–2 hours)
  • Monthly or seasonal treatment options available
Schedule Treatment

Mole Control

Moles can destroy a beautiful lawn overnight. Our mole control program uses proven trapping and treatment methods to remove active moles and protect your turf from damage.

  • Professional mole trapping programs
  • Treatment of grub populations (mole food source)
  • Surface tunnel identification and treatment
  • Damage assessment and follow-up
  • Ongoing monitoring and protection
Schedule Treatment

Armyworm Control

Fall armyworms devastate West Tennessee lawns every late summer. Acting fast is critical — our armyworm treatments stop infestations before they cause lasting damage.

  • Rapid-response insecticide applications
  • Early season inspection and monitoring
  • Targeted treatment of affected lawn areas
  • Follow-up applications as needed
  • Guidance on lawn recovery after infestation
Schedule Treatment

Perimeter Pest Control

Create a barrier around your home that keeps ants, spiders, roaches, and other crawling pests outside where they belong. Our perimeter treatment targets the exterior of your property.

  • Treatment of foundation, entry points & eaves
  • Targets ants, spiders, roaches, centipedes, and more
  • Indoor pest prevention without indoor spraying
  • Applied every 60–90 days for continuous protection
  • Child and pet safe when dry
