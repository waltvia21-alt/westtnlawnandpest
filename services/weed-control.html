Lime Application
Soil Health

Lime Application

Soil pH is the foundation of a healthy lawn. Lime applications neutralize acidic soil so your grass can absorb nutrients effectively.

Most West Tennessee soils trend acidic over time. Without the right pH, even the best fertilizer gets locked out — lime fixes that at the root level.

  • Soil pH testing and analysis
  • Pelletized lime application
  • Improves fertilizer effectiveness
  • Reduces moss and weed pressure
  • Recommended for most West Tennessee soils
