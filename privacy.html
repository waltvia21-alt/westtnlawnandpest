Privacy Policy

How West TN Lawn & Pest collects, uses, and protects your information

Last updated: January 1, 2025

West TN Lawn & Pest ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Name, address, phone number, and email address when you request a quote or schedule service
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment processor — we do not store card numbers)
  • Messages or inquiries submitted through contact forms
  • Account login credentials if you create an account

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search terms that led you to our site
  • Device type and operating system

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, schedule, and invoice lawn care and pest control services
  • Communicate with you about your service, appointment reminders, and follow-ups
  • Send promotional offers or seasonal reminders (you may opt out at any time)
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

SMS / Text Message Communications (TCPA)

By providing your mobile phone number and opting in, you consent to receive recurring automated text messages from West TN Lawn & Pest regarding service reminders, appointment confirmations, promotional offers, and account notifications. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any text message at any time. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent. To opt back in, reply START.

For help: Reply HELP to any message or contact us at info@westtnlawnandpest.com or 731-313-7732.

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Third-party vendors who assist with payment processing, scheduling software, and email delivery — bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and understand where visitors come from. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

We may use Google Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymized data about website usage. Google's privacy policy governs how Google handles that data.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain customer information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations, typically up to 7 years for billing records. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@westtnlawnandpest.com.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
