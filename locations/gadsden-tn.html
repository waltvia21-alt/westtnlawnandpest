Serving Gadsden, TN & Surrounding Rural Crockett County

Gadsden is a small, tightly-knit community within Crockett County where the properties tend to be larger and the pace of life reflects true rural West Tennessee living. With bigger lot sizes come bigger lawn challenges — more ground for weeds to spread, more area requiring consistent fertilization, and often a mix of grass types including both warm-season Bermuda and cool-season Fescue. West TN Lawn & Pest has the equipment and expertise to manage larger rural properties efficiently and cost-effectively.

We know that Gadsden homeowners value results and reliability. When we schedule a visit, we show up — and we treat your lawn with the same care and precision we'd give a small urban yard. Our programs are priced fairly for rural lot sizes, and we work with you to prioritize the areas of your property that need attention most. From county road frontage that gets hammered by roadside weeds to back-lawn Bermuda that needs a boost after summer heat stress, we handle it all.
