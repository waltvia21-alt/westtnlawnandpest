Serving Bells, TN & the Crockett County Community

Bells is the proud county seat of Crockett County — a close-knit small-town community of around 2,400 residents where neighbors know each other and curb appeal matters. The warm, humid summers here are ideal for Bermuda grass, but they also invite aggressive weed pressure and nutrient deficiencies if lawns aren't properly maintained. West TN Lawn & Pest understands the specific soil conditions and weed challenges that Bells homeowners face, and we build every treatment plan around your lawn's actual needs.

From the established residential neighborhoods near downtown Bells to homes on the outskirts of town, we've helped dozens of Crockett County homeowners reclaim their lawns from weeds and achieve the dense, dark-green Bermuda turf that holds up through the hottest West Tennessee summers. We're not a call-center company — we're your neighbors, and we treat your lawn like it's our own.
