Local Experts

Serving Milan, TN with Pride

Milan is one of Gibson County's most storied communities, home to approximately 7,500 residents and the historic Milan Arsenal — one of the oldest army ammunition plants in the United States. The city's tree-lined streets and well-established neighborhoods reflect the pride Milan residents take in their community, and a healthy, well-kept lawn is a big part of that. Whether you live in one of the classic brick-home neighborhoods that have defined Milan for generations or in a newer residential development on the outskirts of town, your lawn has distinct needs that only a locally knowledgeable team can address properly.

Milan's soil conditions, combined with the heat and humidity that blanket West Tennessee from May through September, create ideal conditions for both the Bermuda grass that thrives in full sun and the persistent broadleaf weeds that challenge homeowners every spring. West TN Lawn & Pest applies the right pre-emergent barrier before weed seeds germinate, then follows up with targeted post-emergent treatments throughout the season. Add our fertilization program and your turf gets the steady nutrition it needs to outcompete weeds naturally. Call or request a quote online today.
