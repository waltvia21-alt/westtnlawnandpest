The Story

It Started With My Own Yard

When I moved into my first house here in Jackson, the lawn was a mess — wall-to-wall weeds, clover taking over, grass struggling to survive. Most people would've called someone. I started researching.

With an Agronomy background, I already understood soil, turf, and plant science — but applying that knowledge to a real lawn, in West Tennessee's specific climate, became a passion I didn't expect. I saw what the right products, the right timing, and real attention to detail could do. Within a season, the lawn looked like a different property.

That experience is what West TN Lawn & Pest is built on. Not a franchise. Not a call center. Just someone who genuinely loves this work, knows West Tennessee soils, and takes real pride in the results.

What You Can Expect

The West TN Difference

Personal Walkthrough First

Before any product touches your lawn, I do a personal on-site inspection — identifying weed pressure, soil conditions, and what your turf actually needs. No guessing, no cookie-cutter plans.
